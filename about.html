About Zenith Fashion

Zenith Fashion was established in October 2012 and it started with the sales of shoes. The company came with the goal of delivering the best products to consumers as far as fashion is concerned. The love for Qualityy and our customers desire and demands gave rise to the growth of the company. Today, Zenith Fashion is ranked among the top five fashion companies in Nigeria. We are specialised in mostly female wears of all kinds ranging from Clothes, Jewelries, Bags, Shoes, Make ups and Many more
